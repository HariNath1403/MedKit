Trade Name:

Stugeron

Composition:

Cinnarizine 25mg Tablet

Indication:

Motion sickness or Vertigo

Regimen:

1 tablet (25mg) 2 hours before travelling and 0.5 tablets (12.5mg) every 8 hours during the journey.

Additional Info:

May cause drowsiness. Do not drive or operate heavy machinery.
